Reducing Data Costs

  • • Self-hosting: Tools like farmOS and LiteFarm can be hosted locally by your organization
  • • Offline kits: Kuza and Digital Green offer portable video kits for areas without connectivity
  • • Local downloads: Web Soil Survey and SoilGrids data can be downloaded once for offline reference
  • • SMS-based tools: KAOP and iShamba work via SMS without data plans

Next Steps

  • • Ask: Can you export your data if you leave?
  • • Check: Who controls this tool and your information?
