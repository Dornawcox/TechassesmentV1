Understanding ETA Scores

Scored Dimensions (25 points each)

  • Data Ethics — consent, privacy, minimization
  • Interoperability — APIs, standards, export
  • Commons Fit — license, governance, contribution
  • Accessibility — languages, bandwidth, offline

CEI Fit Levels

  • High Fully aligned with commons principles
  • Medium-High Mostly aligned, minor concerns
  • Medium Some alignment, notable trade-offs
  • Low Limited alignment, significant concerns
